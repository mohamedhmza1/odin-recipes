pasta Recipe

Description

This homemade pasta recipe is easy, delicious, and comforting. Start by boiling pasta in salted water until tender. While it cooks, prepare a rich tomato or creamy Alfredo sauce. Once the pasta is drained, toss it with the sauce and finish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and herbs. Simple and satisfying, this pasta makes a perfect weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce or cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Basil or parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Water in a pot and add salt
  2. Cook Pasta under tender, then drain
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic
  4. Add tomato sauce or cream to make the sauce
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce
  6. Additional:Top with parmesan cheese and herbs before serving
