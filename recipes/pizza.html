Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is simple, flavorful, and customizable to your taste. Start with a soft, chewy crust, add a layer of tangy tomato sauce, and top it with gooey mozzarella cheese. From there, you can add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or fresh vegetables. Baked until golden and bubbling, this pizza is perfect or a quick family dinner or a weekend treat.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Preheat the oven
  3. Shape the Dough
  4. Add Sauce
  5. Add Cheese
  6. Optional:Add Toppings
  7. Bake the pizza
  8. Finish and serve
