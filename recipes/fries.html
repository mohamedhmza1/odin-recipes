Fries Recipe

Description

Crispy, golden fries are a classic comfort food enjoyed all over the world. Made from simple ingredients, they’re easy to prepare and can be served as a snack or a side dish. When cooked properly, fries have a crunchy exterior and a soft, fluffy inside, making them the perfect treat with ketchup, mayo, or your favorite dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes.
  2. Cut the potatoes into thin, even strips.
  3. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a paper towel.
  5. Heat the oil in a deep pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry the potatoes in batches for 4–5 minutes until lightly golden.
  7. Remove and let them cool for a few minutes.
  8. Increase the oil temperature to 375°F (190°C) and fry the potatoes again until crispy and golden brown.
  9. Drain the fries on paper towels and season with salt.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
